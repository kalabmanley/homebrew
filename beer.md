## Oregon IPA : [5gallons batch]
1. 6pounds two row barley.
2. 2pounds wheat.
3. 1lb 60L crystal malt.
4. 1pound vienna malt.
 * Steep two minimum with dextrose.
5. 1ounce raw Cascade Hops.
 * Alpha: 7.7%
 * Beta: 6.9%
6. 1ounce Nugget Millenium.
 * Alpha: 15.9%
 * Beta: 5.0%
7. 1ounce Chinook Raw Hops.
 * Alpha: 13.6%
 * Beta: 3.6%
